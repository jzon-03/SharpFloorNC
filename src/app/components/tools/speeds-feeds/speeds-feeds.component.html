Formula Reference

  • RPM = (SFM × 12) ÷ (π × Diameter)
  • Feed Rate = RPM × Chipload × Flutes
  • SFM = (RPM × π × Diameter) ÷ 12
  • Material Removal = Width × Depth × Feed

General Guidelines

  • Start Conservative - 75% of recommended speeds
  • Increase Gradually - Monitor chip formation
  • Proper Coolant - Essential for tool life
  • Machine Rigidity - Affects achievable parameters

Troubleshooting

  • Chatter - Reduce speed or increase feed
  • Poor Finish - Check chipload and rigidity
  • Tool Wear - Reduce speed, increase feed
  • Built-up Edge - Increase speed or coolant
