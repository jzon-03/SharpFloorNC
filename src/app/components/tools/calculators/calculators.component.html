info Common Formulas

Surface Speed: SFM = (π × D × RPM) ÷ 12
RPM: RPM = (SFM × 12) ÷ (π × D)
Feed Rate: IPM = RPM × Chipload × Flutes
Chipload: IPT = IPM ÷ (RPM × Flutes)
MRR: in³/min = Axial × Radial × Feed
