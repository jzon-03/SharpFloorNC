Machinability Rating: Based on 360 Brass (100). Higher numbers = easier to machine.

tips_and_updates Machining Tips

Work Hardening: Stainless steels and some alloys work harden rapidly.
Built-up Edge: Common with aluminum and soft steels at low speeds.
Tool Selection: Match tool material to workpiece for optimal results.
Coolant Choice: Critical for tool life and surface finish quality.

security Safety Considerations

Titanium: Fire hazard with chips. Use flood coolant and proper chip management.
Cutting Oils: Fire hazard, proper ventilation required.
Material Handling: Some alloys have sharp edges and toxic elements.
Coolant Safety: Proper mixing ratios and maintenance prevent health issues.
